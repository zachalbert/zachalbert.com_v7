I'm a digital product designer and entrepreneur, and I spend most of my time mentoring and developing designers. I’ve spent the last running the product design program at Tradecraft, and have trained over 350 designers who now work all over the world in places like Google, Netflix, Tesla, Samsung and Facebook. We have good reviews.

Prior to that, I worked for over 10 years as a consultant and first product designer / UX designer for many early stage startups, larger orgs like Target, and as a cofounder of my own startup built to help fine artists. I've done everything from graphic design, to 3D modeling, to product management, but I'm happiest when doing end-to-end design and hand coding prototypes alongside developers, researchers, and product managers.

I train designers to be resilient, able to get work done, etc. I ask designers to pursue the same principles I pursue for myself, namely that designers should…

  • … work on things that should exist
  • … look out for each other
  • … be autodidacts
  • … be aggressively curious
  • … be resilient AF
  • … be systems thinkers
  • … be disciplined
